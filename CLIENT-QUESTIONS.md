## Donate flow

The "Make a Donation" button currently links to `#`. Two paths forward:

**Option A — Accept donations directly on the site.**
We can wire the button to a working donation flow (Squarespace's built-in
donation block, or Stripe/PayPal). This requires the foundation to set up
**merchant services** first (bank/processor account, fee structure, receipt
templates, 501(c)(3) confirmation for the donor's tax purposes).

**Option B — Redirect to an intake form for now.**
If the foundation isn't ready to set up merchant services yet, the Donate
button can point to a short intake form ("Tell us a bit about how you'd like
to give"). Submissions land in the foundation's inbox and someone calls the
donor back to arrange the gift directly. Lower friction to launch; preserves
the personal-touch quality of the foundation's work.

**Option C — Mailing address only.**
The button links to a page that says "We accept checks at this address."
Simplest of all; appropriate if 95% of giving is going to come from people
who already know the family.

Please indicate which option to wire up. We can also stage Option B now and
upgrade to Option A once merchant services are in place.
